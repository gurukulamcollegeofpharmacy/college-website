Diploma in Pharmacy (D.Pharm) — 2 Year Program

About the Course

The Diploma in Pharmacy (D.Pharm) is a 2-year foundational qualification designed for students who want to start a career in pharmaceutical sciences and pharmacy practice.

Qualification & Duration

  • Program: Diploma in Pharmacy (D.Pharm)
  • Duration: 2 Years
  • Level: Entry-level professional pharmacy qualification

Eligibility Criteria

Passed HSC (10+2) or its equivalent examination with English as one of the subjects. Physics and Chemistry are compulsory subjects along with Mathematics or Biology as optional subjects individually.
